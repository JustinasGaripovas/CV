Personal Values

Improvement

Let's start from Improvement (beforementioned wish to grow). In my opinion the most important value for each and every developer or professional of their craft. Constant wish to better yourself, discover something new and exciting is the essence of my character.

Optimization

Optimization, if that could be called value is highly underestimated. In my worldview, doing something more efficiently with less energy wasted is the goal. The goal that everyone should strive to achieve. From extensive use of shortcuts when developing, to basic rules when cleaning your room. Having a mindset with optimization at its core brought more satisfying living style.

Teamwork

Teamwork, connections between people, finding the right, like-minded group of coworkers is the key to successful working experience. My ideal working environment is where you give and receive a helping hand, where there is a sense of promoting and pushing each other's skills and ideas, not ignoring them or dragging them through the mud.
